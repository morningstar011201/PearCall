Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GhostCall is designed with privacy at its core. We collect minimal data, store no call audio, and never require you to create an account.

1. Who We Are

GhostCall is a free peer-to-peer voice calling service available at ghostcall.space. We are an independent project.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how GhostCall is used. This includes:

  • Anonymous device ID (randomly generated, stored in your browser)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, or tablet)
  • Country (detected via Cloudflare's network)
  • Event types (session started, room created, call connected, call ended)
  • Call duration (in seconds)
  • Room ID (randomly generated, not linked to you)

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Your phone number or contacts
  • Any call audio or conversation content
  • Your precise location or IP address
  • Any data that can identify you personally

3. How Calls Work

GhostCall uses WebRTC technology for peer-to-peer audio. This means your voice travels directly between devices — it does not pass through our servers. We cannot listen to, record, or store your calls.

When you hang up, the room code becomes invalid and is permanently deleted. There is no call history.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to store a randomly generated anonymous device ID. This contains no personal information and is used only to count unique sessions in our analytics. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

5. Third Party Services

GhostCall uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — stores anonymous analytics events.
  • Cloudflare — provides TURN server relay for calls and detects country.
  • PeerJS — handles WebRTC connection signaling between devices.
  • Vercel — hosts the GhostCall website.
  • Google Fonts — serves the font used in our interface.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics events are retained indefinitely for product improvement. Active room records are automatically deleted within 30 minutes of a call ending.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete that is linked to you. Your anonymous device ID can be cleared by clearing your browser's local storage.

8. Children's Privacy

GhostCall does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The service is intended for general audiences.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at ghostcall.space.
